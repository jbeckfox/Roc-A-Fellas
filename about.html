At Rocafella's Pizza we hand make our own dough, fresh daily - right here in our own kitchen. It's our own authentic New York recipe.

Each morning we blend our homemade, special New York sauce, made with crushed tomatoes, garlic, authentic Italian spices, real Romano cheese, and our tasty secret – a dash of sour cream! Mmmm yummy.

We hand-toss the dough to make every pizza in the true New York style – Thinner hand–tossed pizza with light sauce and a crisped bottom. Then we cover it with toppings made fresh daily using authentic New York recipes and the finest ingredients available.

Then we bake your pizza in a genuine stone oven for that wonderful “Big Apple” flavor.

You can buy it by the slice (big enough to fold) or get whole pies.

And not only that, we make hoagies, sandwiches, and salads – all made to order the way you want.

For a true “Taste of New York” come in today! We are located in Sharonville, in the Square, next to the Post Office – plenty of parking in rear of our building.
